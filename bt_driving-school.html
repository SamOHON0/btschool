About BT School of Motoring

A patient, professional approach

Richie Brophy is the owner and manager of BT School of Motoring, operating from a base in Celbridge, Co. Kildare and serving the areas of Lucan, Celbridge, Clane, Leixlip, Maynooth and Dublin West. Richie is a qualified driving instructor with the Road Safety Authority, registration No. 41290, and holds a Fetac Level 6 certificate in Driving Tuition.

Richie first entered the haulage business in 1995 and in later years became a driver trainer, driver assessor and defensive driving instructor. He went on to become a manual handling risk assessor, carrying out risk assessments, delivery point risk assessments and accident and incident investigations.

Richie's love for all things driving inspired him to become an instructor, and he likes nothing more than to see his clients pass their test. BT School of Motoring currently hold a 98% overall driving test pass rate.

We look forward to hearing from you, and indeed to adding you to our long list of successful clients over the past many years, all of whom have passed their driving test and are driving safely with skill and competence on the roads today.

Thank you for visiting our website.
Richie Brophy, RSA ADI No. 41290
