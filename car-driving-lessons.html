Car Lessons & EDT

Your step by step path to passing the test

Here at BT School of Motoring we specialise in pre-tests with above average pass rates. You will learn using the most modern teaching methods in a fully insured, dual control tuition vehicle with an RSA approved and registered driving instructor who holds a Fetac Level 6 qualification in driving tuition. Richie may also act as your EDT sponsor.

In addition to teaching you to drive, we also offer driver training in your own vehicle, including pre-test lessons prior to your driving test in manual or automatic. We offer the flexibility of lessons when and where you want them, with no long-term commitment to make.
