About BT School of Motoring

Meet your instructor, Richie Brophy

Richie Brophy is the owner and manager of BT School of Motoring, operating from a base in Celbridge, Co. Kildare and serving the areas of Lucan, Celbridge, Clane, Leixlip, Maynooth and Dublin West. Richie is a qualified driving instructor with the Road Safety Authority, registration No. 41290, and holds a Fetac Level 6 certificate in Driving Tuition. For the protection of all our clients, he is also qualified in licence categories A-Motorcycle, B-Cars and Vans, BE-Jeep and Trailer, C-Rigid Trucks, CE-Articulated Trucks and Fork-Lift Trucks.

Richie first entered the haulage business in 1995 and in later years became a driver trainer, driver assessor and defensive driving instructor, furthering his career as a qualified RTITB fork truck instructor. He went on to become a qualified driver and manual handling risk assessor, carrying out risk assessments, delivery point risk assessments and accident and incident investigations.

Richie's love for all things driving inspired him to become an instructor, and he likes nothing more than to see his clients pass their test. BT School of Motoring currently hold a 98% overall driving test pass rate.

We look forward to hearing from you, and indeed to adding you to our long list of successful clients over the past many years, all of whom have passed their driving test and are driving safely with skill and competence on the roads today.
