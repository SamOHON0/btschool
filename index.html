About BT School of Motoring

Meet your instructor, Richie Brophy

Richie Brophy is the owner and manager of BT School of Motoring, operating from a base in Celbridge, Co. Kildare and serving Lucan, Celbridge, Clane, Leixlip, Maynooth and Dublin West. Richie is a qualified RSA driving instructor, registration No. 41290, and holds a Fetac Level 6 certificate in Driving Tuition.

Richie likes nothing more than to see his clients pass their test. BT School of Motoring currently hold a 98% overall driving test pass rate.

For the protection of all our clients, Richie is also qualified in licence categories A-Motorcycle, B-Cars and Vans, BE-Jeep and Trailer, C-Rigid Trucks, CE-Articulated Trucks and Fork-Lift Trucks.

How it works

A step by step approach to confidence building and driver competence, with no long-term commitment along the way.

01

Get in touch

Call us to arrange your first lesson when and where it suits you. Early booking is advised, and there is no long-term commitment to make.

02

Work through your EDT

Follow our easy, step by step EDT course using modern teaching methods in a fully insured, dual control tuition vehicle. Richie may also act as your EDT sponsor.

03

Prepare with a pre-test

A two hour pre-test in your car or ours is recommended, and includes clear feedback alongside a completed pre-test marking sheet.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

A one hour driving lesson is €60. A 12 hour driving lesson or EDT package is €660. The official RSA EDT log book is €5, with lessons also uploaded to the RSA portal.

No long-term commitment is necessary. Your first lesson may be undertaken whenever you wish, and thereafter you simply book a further lesson or lessons on conclusion. You can book in one or two hour slots, lesson by lesson, or in pre-booked groups.

Cancelling a driving lesson isn't an issue with 48 hours notice. This enables us to offer the cancelled time slot to another client.
