Towing & Trailer Tuition

Confident, safe towing for jeep, car & van

If you need to tow a trailer, horsebox, plant, caravan or boat, the right tuition makes all the difference. BT School of Motoring offers jeep and trailer lessons in the BE licence category, as well as trailer tuition for car and van drivers, taught by a qualified RSA instructor.

Lessons run in two hour slots, with full lesson packages available. Own car, jeep and trailer lessons can be arranged on request, subject to terms and conditions, and we can arrange a suitable meeting point to suit you.
