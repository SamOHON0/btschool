Truck & HGV Tuition

Learn to drive a truck and change your career

Thinking about a career change? Rigid truck lessons are available with BT School of Motoring, taught by owner Richie Brophy, a qualified RSA driving instructor and RTITB fork truck instructor with a background in haulage since 1995, driver training, driver assessment and defensive driving.

Whether you are an individual starting out behind the wheel of a truck for the first time, or an employer who needs drivers trained and assessed, we will build a programme around you. Lessons are delivered in two hour slots, and a truck can be made available for the driving test, subject to availability and terms and conditions.
